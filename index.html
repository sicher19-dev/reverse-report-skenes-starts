Recurring Tendencies

The two pitches that cost him

2nd inning

Mistake 1: the sinker that stayed up

To Hernández, a 96-mph sinker that was supposed to live at the knees instead caught the middle of the plate at 2.2 feet — belt-high, over the heart (xBA .76). The sinker only works down and arm-side; up and middle, it’s a fastball with less ride. 104 off the bat, 403 feet. Finish it down.

2nd inning

Mistake 2: the changeup that hung

To Mack, two outs, a changeup that floated to 2.6 feet — the top of the zone. A changeup belongs at or below the knees as a chase/whiff pitch; up there it’s the easiest pitch in baseball to hit. 102 off the bat, 424 feet. The more glaring of the two. Bury it.

34.6 CSW

Everything else missed bats

Outside those two pitches, this was a swing-and-miss masterclass: 23 whiffs, 10 K, struck out the side in the 4th and the 6th. The four-seam, sweeper and splitter were all chase-and-miss weapons. The stuff was never the issue — it was overwhelming.

+1.62 → −3.07

Front-loaded, then untouchable

All of the run-value damage was the first time through (+1.62, i.e. the two homers). Across the second and third trips he was −3.07 combined, retiring 13 of his last 14 with the only baserunner a stranded single. He didn’t fade — he locked in.

The Bottom Line

An ace night, two pitches short of a gem

This is the cleanest kind of report to act on, because the to-do list is two pitches long. The stuff, the misses, the efficiency, the finish — all elite. The loss and the two earned runs trace entirely to a sinker and a changeup that didn’t get to their spots, in the same inning.

Keep

Essentially everything. A 34.6% CSW, 10 K, striking out the side twice, and a −1.45 run value against a big-league lineup is a front-of-rotation night. There’s no command-wide or sequencing problem here.

Fix

Two locations. The sinker has to finish down (it died at 2.2 ft, middle); the changeup has to be buried (it hung at 2.6 ft). Both were elevated arm-side mistakes in the 2nd. Land those two and this is a two-hit, double-digit-strikeout shutout.
